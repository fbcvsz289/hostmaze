## What's Actually Included on Every Plan?

Whether you're picking up a €2/month shared plan or a €40/month KVM VPS, a few things are standard across the board:

**Virtualizor Control Panel** — for VPS plans, this lets you start/stop/reboot, manage rDNS, run backups, and reinstall your OS without waiting on support. You do it yourself, instantly.

**DDoS Protection** — hardware-level DDoS mitigation on all plans. VPS plans use advanced Layer 7 filtering, dedicated servers use NSFOCUS equipment.

**Unmetered Bandwidth** — no traffic caps. That's a notable advantage over providers who meter at, say, 1–2TB and charge overages.

**Free Daily Backups** — included on VPS plans. That's not always a given at these price points.

**Instant Deployment** — automated provisioning typically under 5 minutes after payment clears.

**Allowed Use Cases** — VPNs, IRC servers, Tor nodes, GRE tunneling, torrents. All ports open. That's explicitly stated, which saves you the headache of finding out after you've signed up that your use case is blocked.
